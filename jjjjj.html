Our Services

Okla Natural Honey

Okla Natural Honey is a Kenyan enterprise aiming to be the leading provider of high-quality, natural bee products. They achieve this by empowering farmers, particularly in Trans Nzoia's Cherangany region, through sustainable beekeeping training, resources, and reliable market access. Beyond promoting pure honey production, the company actively drives environmental conservation and seeks to diversify agricultural activities. Their commitment extends to securing vital partnerships for sustained business growth and community development across Kenya.

About Okla Natural Honey

Okla Natural Honey empowers farmers in Cherangany, Trans Nzoia County through sustainable beekeeping. We mobilize and train farmers, providing affordable beehives, setup, harvesting assistance, and long-term contracts for reliable income.
