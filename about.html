About Okla Natural Honey

Okla Natural Honey empowers farmers in Cherangany, Trans Nzoia County through sustainable beekeeping. We mobilize and train farmers, providing affordable beehives, setup, harvesting assistance, and long-term contracts for reliable income.

Vision

“To be the leading provider of high-quality, natural bee products in Kenya while empowering farmers through sustainable beekeeping, environmental conservation, and economic growth.”

Mission

“At Okla Natural Honey, we are committed to promoting sustainable beekeeping by providing high-quality beehives, training, and market access to farmers. We strive to produce and supply pure, natural bee products while enhancing environmental conservation and improving livelihoods through fair trade and economic empowerment.”

Goals & Objectives

  • Promote Sustainable Beekeeping: Mobilize and train farmers to adopt beekeeping as a profitable and eco-friendly venture.
  • Enhance Honey Production & Quality: Ensure high-quality, natural honey through training and quality control.
  • Expand Market Access: Provide reliable market access and connect farmers to buyers.
  • Support Farmers: Offer beehives, tools, and continuous training.
  • Environmental Conservation: Encourage tree planting and sustainable land management.
  • Diversify Agriculture: Integrate beekeeping with other agricultural activities.
  • Secure Partnerships: Work with investors and NGOs to grow the initiative.
  • Ensure Sustainability: Scale up honey production and expand market reach.

Okla Natural Honey is a duly registered company with its headquarters in Cherangany Constituency and mainly catering for Bee Farmers in Trans-Nzoia - Cherangany.
